Common Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Send us an email.

What is Flinker and how does it transform project management?

Flinker Apps are Microsoft 365 integrated BIM Apps for open standards such as IFC, BCF or IDS. Self-hosted within your Microsoft tenant, modular, and API-first - unifying communication, BIM setups, plan management, and quality control within Microsoft Teams and SharePoint.

Is Flinker suitable for all project stakeholders?

Yes. Flinker Apps are designed for project owners, planners, contractors, and real estate operators - streamlining communication and data exchange at every stage. Work securely with external stakeholders using granular permission controls.

Do Flinker Apps integrate with our existing systems?

Yes. Deep integrations with Microsoft 365 including Power Automate, Power BI, Microsoft Azure, and Microsoft Copilot - plus robust APIs. All built on open standards like IFC and BCF.

How do I get started with Flinker?

Start with the free Open IFC Viewer from Microsoft AppSource. For full deployments, contact our team for a consultation, or find a certified partner in your region.

Can I get help from a local partner?

Yes. We have certified partners across Europe, Asia, and the Americas trained on our playbooks and quality standards. Find a partner or become one.
