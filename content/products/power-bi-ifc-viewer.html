Can I try the IFC Viewer before paying?

Yes. The Free plan lets you use the IFC Viewer in Power BI Desktop with a starter template. You only need to pay when unlocking Pro features or enabling sharing in the Power BI Service.

How do I purchase Flinker Power BI licenses?

You have two options:

1) Single-user licenses (self-service via Microsoft AppSource)
Buy seats in Microsoft AppSource. Billing runs through your Microsoft purchase flow, and your Microsoft 365 admin assigns the licenses to the right users.

2) Tenant-wide licenses (covers everyone, no assignments)
For tenant-wide licensing, contact us at sales@flinker.app (or book a call). Tenant-wide licensing automatically covers all users in your tenant, so no one needs to assign licenses to individuals.

Is pricing per user?

Our main plans are billed per tenant (no per‚Äëuser fees). For small pilots, you can also purchase single-user licenses via Microsoft AppSource.

What is per-tenant pricing?

A tenant is your organization‚Äôs dedicated Microsoft 365 environment ‚Äî your users, groups, domains, security policies, and SharePoint/Teams data. Our pricing applies to your tenant as a whole.

How is the Pro plan billed?

The Pro plan is billed per tenant at a flat monthly rate. All editors within your tenant get access to Pro features ‚Äî there‚Äôs no per-user fee.

Do viewers also need a license?

No. Viewers only require a license once you enable Power BI Service sharing. In the Free and Pro plans, only editors in Desktop are covered.

What payment methods do you support?

We support credit card payments via Stripe and invoice billing for Business and Enterprise customers.
