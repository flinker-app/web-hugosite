Partnership Tiers

Choose the partnership level that fits your business. Progress through tiers based on certifications, deal activity, and engagement.

Referral Partner

Entry level for consultants and advisors who recommend Flinker to their clients.

  • Referral commissions on closed deals
  • Access to Partner Portal
  • Co-branded marketing materials
  • Partner Directory listing
Most Popular

Service Partner

For consultancies and integrators who implement and support Flinker solutions.

  • All Referral Partner benefits
  • NFR licenses for demos and training
  • Implementation playbooks and templates
  • Co-selling support on opportunities
  • Technical enablement and certification

Strategic Partner

Deep collaboration with joint go-to-market activities and business planning.

  • All Service Partner benefits
  • Dedicated Partner Manager
  • Joint marketing campaigns
  • Early access to product updates
  • Annual business planning
