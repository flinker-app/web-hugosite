The Problem with Standalone CDEs

Tools like Dalux, ACC Docs, or Box solve document management — but add another platform, another login, and another data silo to your IT landscape.

Fragmented IT

Yet another platform to manage, secure, and pay for — separate from your Microsoft 365 tenant.

Data Governance Risk

Project files leave your tenant. Compliance teams need to audit yet another vendor's security posture.

User Adoption Gap

Teams already work in SharePoint and Teams. A separate CDE means training, context-switching, and low adoption.

The Flinker Approach

Your CDE Runs on SharePoint

Instead of replacing SharePoint with a construction-specific tool, Flinker extends SharePoint into a full CDE with IFC viewing and ISO 19650 workflows.

IFC Viewer

Open IFC files directly from SharePoint libraries. View, filter, measure — without leaving your browser.

ISO 19650

Manage suitability codes, status, revisions, and approval workflows — mapped to ISO 19650 standards.

Self-Hosted

All data stays in your Microsoft 365 tenant. No external servers, no file transfers, full compliance.

Familiar UX

Teams already know SharePoint and Teams. No training needed, no new login, instant adoption.
