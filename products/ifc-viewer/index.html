Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find an answer? Contact support

Is this an online IFC viewer if files stay on my device?

Yes. The viewer runs in your browser but processes files locally on your device by default. Your model data never leaves your computer unless you explicitly save to SharePoint or Teams.

Which IFC versions are supported?

We support IFC 2x3, IFC 4, and IFC 4x3 with accelerated rendering optimized for enterprise-scale datasets.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free gives you full access to view, filter, and analyze IFC models — forever, no limits. Pro adds cloud save features: save Excel and BCF files directly to SharePoint or Teams, and sync BCF issues to Microsoft Lists for workflow automation. One Pro license works across all M365 apps.

How do I install the app in Teams or SharePoint?

Get it from Microsoft AppSource and add it to your Teams channel or SharePoint site. No admin approval needed for most organizations. See our setup guide for step-by-step instructions.

Can I embed the IFC Viewer in my platform?

Yes. Enterprise licensing is available for embedding the viewer in your CDE, digital twin, or custom application. White-label options available. Contact us to discuss.
