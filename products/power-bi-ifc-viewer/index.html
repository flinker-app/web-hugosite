FAQs

Can’t find an answer?
Ask our customer support.

Can I try the IFC Viewer before paying?

Yes. The Free plan lets you use the IFC Viewer in Power BI Desktop with a starter template. You only need to pay when unlocking Pro features or enabling sharing in the Power BI Service.

How is the Pro plan billed?

The Pro plan is billed per tenant at a flat monthly rate. All editors within your tenant get access to Pro features — there’s no per-user fee.

Do viewers also need a license?

No. Viewers only require a license once you enable Power BI Service sharing. In the Free and Pro plans, only editors in Desktop are covered.

What payment methods do you support?

We support credit card payments via Stripe and invoice billing for Business and Enterprise customers.
