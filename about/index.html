Our Mission

Robotize buildings and infrastructure

We are Flinker, accelerating human to robot collaboration for construction, property and facility management processes to protect and empower humans.

About the Movement

We’re shaping the OpenBIM ecosystem for Microsoft 365 — a connected, self-hosted environment where companies, integrators, and developers can build, connect, and innovate together using open standards like IFC, IDS, and BCF.

Our goal is to create a transparent, collaborative foundation for digital construction and facility management — keeping data ownership with users while enabling faster, more open innovation across the built environment.
