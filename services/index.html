What is Flinker and how does it transform project management?

Flinker Apps are Microsoft 365 integrated BIM Apps for open standards such as IFC, BCF or IDS. Self-hosted within your Microsoft tenant, modular, and API-first - unifying communication, BIM setups, plan management, and quality control within Microsoft Teams and SharePoint for seamless project collaboration.

Is Flinker suitable for all project stakeholders?

Yes. Flinker Microsoft Apps are designed for project owners, planners, contractors, and real estate operators - streamlining communication and data exchange at every stage of your project. Work securely with external stakeholders using granular permission controls.

Do Flinker Apps integrate with our existing systems?

Absolutely. With deep integrations in Microsoft 365 including Power Automate, Power BI, Microsoft Azure, and Microsoft Copilot - plus robust APIs - our apps easily integrate with your existing workflows. All built on open standards like IFC and BCF.

How do I get started with Flinker?

Start with the free Open IFC Viewer from Microsoft AppSource. For full deployments, contact our team for a personalized consultation, or find a certified partner in your region.

Can I get help from a local partner?

Yes. We have a growing network of certified implementation partners across Europe, Asia, and the Americas. Partners are non-exclusive and trained on our playbooks and quality standards. Find a partner or become one.
